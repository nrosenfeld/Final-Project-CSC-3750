Leap Ahead Summer Academy

Who Are We?

Leap Ahead Summer Academy is a summer school for high-schoolers which is designed to make summer school enjoyable. We balance a couple of hours of classes in the morning with access to a variety of healthy and supervised recreational activities. The combination of both keeps young men and women coming willingly and gives them a boost on the upcoming year!

What scholarships are available?

We do offer need-based scholarships. On the application form, click the box that indicates that you are requesting a scholarship and we will contact you about it. Besides the scholarships that we offer, there are many available from several community organizations.

  • The United Way of St Louis County
  • The Community Action Agency of St. Louis County
  • Area Resources for Community and Human Services (ARCHES)
  • St. Louis County Board of Education
  • St. Louis Community Foundation
If you are applying for a scholarship, either from us or from any other organization, it is best to apply first so that you know how much the amount is that needs to be subsidized. Don't worry, filling out the form doesn't obligate you to pay anything. You owe nothing until enrollment is complete.
